Frequently Asked Questions About TMJ/TMD Treatment

How much does TMJ/TMD treatment typically cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
Treatment costs vary depending on the severity of your condition and required interventions. Initial consultations are typically $175-225. Basic TMJ treatment plans including custom night guards and therapy start around $800-1,200. More comprehensive treatment involving orthodontic intervention or advanced procedures can range from $2,500-5,000. We accept most major insurance plans and offer flexible payment options through CareCredit. We'll provide a detailed cost breakdown after your initial evaluation.
How long does TMJ/TMD treatment usually take to show results?
Most patients begin experiencing relief from TMJ pain within 2-4 weeks of starting treatment. However, complete treatment duration varies by case. Conservative treatments like night guards and exercises typically show significant improvement within 2-3 months. More complex cases requiring orthodontic intervention may take 6-12 months. We create individualized treatment timelines during your consultation and adjust as needed based on your progress during regular follow-up appointments.
What types of TMJ/TMD treatments do you offer at your New Canaan location?
We offer a comprehensive range of TMJ/TMD treatments including: custom-fitted night guards and splints, trigger point therapy, ultrasound treatment, TENS (transcutaneous electrical nerve stimulation), jaw exercises and physical therapy, orthodontic alignment when needed, and Botox therapy for muscle tension. Our treatment approach is conservative, starting with non-invasive options before considering more advanced interventions. Dr. Gardner has over 15 years of experience treating TMJ disorders and is certified in both traditional and innovative treatment methods.

What happens during the first TMJ consultation appointment?
Your initial consultation lasts approximately 60-90 minutes. We begin with a detailed medical history review and discussion of your symptoms.
