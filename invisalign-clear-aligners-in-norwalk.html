How long does the Invisalign treatment process take?
Most of our Norwalk patients complete their Invisalign treatment in 12-18 months. However, treatment time varies based on individual needs - minor alignment issues may take as little as 6 months, while more complex cases could take up to 24 months. We use advanced 3D scanning technology to create a customized treatment plan during your first visit, which will give you a precise timeline. For best results, aligners should be worn 20-22 hours per day, only removing them to eat, drink, brush and floss.
What happens during the initial Invisalign consultation at your office?
Your first visit to our Stamford office takes about 60 minutes. Dr. Garcia will examine your teeth and take digital 3D scans (no messy impressions needed). We'll show you a virtual preview of your projected results using our iTero scanner, discuss your treatment goals, and create a customized plan. We'll also review costs, insurance coverage, and payment options. The consultation is completely free, and there's no obligation to proceed with treatment. We encourage you to bring any questions you have about the process.
