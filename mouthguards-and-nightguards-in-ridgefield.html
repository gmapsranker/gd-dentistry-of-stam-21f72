Frequently Asked Questions About Mouthguards and Nightguards

How much do custom nightguards cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford?
Our custom-fitted nightguards typically range from $400-600, depending on the specific material and thickness needed for your situation. This includes the initial consultation, dental impressions, fitting appointment, and any minor adjustments needed within the first 3 months. We accept most major insurance plans, which may cover a portion of the cost, and we offer CareCredit financing options to help make treatment more affordable.
How long does it take to get a custom mouthguard made?
The entire process usually takes 2-3 weeks from start to finish. At your first appointment (30-45 minutes), we'll take digital impressions of your teeth. These are sent to our local dental lab in Connecticut, where your custom guard is fabricated. Once ready (typically 10-14 business days), you'll return for a fitting appointment (20-30 minutes) where we ensure proper fit and comfort, making any necessary adjustments.
What's the difference between getting a mouthguard from GD Dentistry versus a store-bought one?
While over-the-counter mouthguards cost less initially, custom guards from our office provide superior protection and comfort. We create an exact fit using precise dental impressions, ensuring proper bite alignment and even pressure distribution. Our guards are made from higher-grade materials that last 3-5 years with proper care, compared to 3-6 months for store-bought options. Custom guards are also thinner and more comfortable, making you more likely to wear them consistently.
Do you offer sports mouthguards for children in Ridgefield?
Yes, we specialize in custom sports mouthguards for children and teenagers in the Ridgefield area. Our guards are particularly popular among students playing for local schools and sports leagues. We offer various color options and can accommodate braces if needed. For growing children, we recommend annual evaluations to ensure proper fit, and we provide special rates for replacement guards as children grow.
What if my nightguard doesn't fit correctly or is uncomfortable?
We guarantee the fit of all our custom guards. If you experience any discomfort or fit issues within the first 3 months, we'll adjust or remake your guard at no additional cost. During your fitting appointment, Dr. Dental will ensure proper fit and provide care instructions. We recommend bringing your guard to your regular check-ups so we can verify it's still fitting and functioning correctly. Most adjustments can be made same-day during your appointment.
