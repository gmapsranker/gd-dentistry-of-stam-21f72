Frequently Asked Questions About Root Canal Therapy

How much does a root canal typically cost at GD Dentistry of Stamford?

How long does a root canal procedure take at your office?
Most root canals at GD Dentistry can be completed in a single 90-minute appointment. However, more complex cases may require two visits spaced about a week apart. During your consultation, we'll outline your specific treatment timeline. We reserve dedicated time slots for root canal procedures, so you won't feel rushed, and we're located conveniently off I-95 in Darien for easy access from surrounding Fairfield County communities.
Will my root canal hurt? What type of anesthesia do you use?
Our priority is keeping you comfortable throughout the procedure. We use powerful local anesthesia (typically Septocaine) to completely numb the area. Many patients report that a root canal feels similar to getting a filling. Dr. Gardner has over 15 years of experience in endodontic procedures and uses the latest rotary instruments which help make the process more comfortable. We also offer nitrous oxide sedation for anxious patients at no additional charge.
What is your success rate for root canals, and do you offer any guarantees?
Our practice has a 95% success rate for root canal procedures. Dr. Gardner uses advanced 3D imaging technology to ensure precise treatment.
